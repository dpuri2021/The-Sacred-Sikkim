    Iconic Monasteries to Showcase

    Rumtek Monastery

    A sacred Tibetan Buddhist monastery known for its magnificent architecture and spiritual festivals.

    Enchey Monastery

    A peaceful retreat offering breathtaking views and insights into Sikkim's religious heritage.

    Pemayangtse Monastery

    Noted for its thangka paintings and architectural marvels in the heart of Sikkim's landscapes.

    Tashiding Monastery

    A place of profound peace and spirituality, home to ancient relics and colorful festivals.

    Phodong Monastery

    The second-largest monastery in Sikkim, steeped in history and panoramic mountain vistas.

    Cultural Preservation Efforts

    Our initiative digitizes monasteries to promote tourism while safeguarding Sikkim's Buddhist cultural heritage. Through virtual tours, educational content, and conservation programs, we ensure these sacred sites endure for future generations.
